Tank Arena - Weapon SFX Search / Generation Prompts

Project context:
Real-time multiplayer top-down tank arena game. Backend is authoritative. Frontend renders server state. Visual style is arcade, neon-accented, readable, fast, and not hyper-realistic.

General audio direction:
Use short, punchy, dry sounds with clear gameplay readability. Avoid long cinematic tails, music, voices, or heavy ambience. Sounds should work in repeated multiplayer combat without fatigue.
